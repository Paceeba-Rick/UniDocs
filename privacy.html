UniDocs Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

UniDocs, a Ghanaian-based document retrieval service, is committed to protecting the personal information of our clients.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data in accordance with the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) of Ghana.

2. Personal Data Collection

We collect personal data from clients when they:

- Request document retrieval services through our platform.

- Create an account with us

- Contact our customer support team

The personal data we collect may include:

- Name

- Email address

- Phone number

- University name

- Student ID number

- Document type

3. Purpose of Personal Data Collection

We collect personal data to:

- Process document retrieval requests efficiently

- Deliver documents to clients promptly

- Improve our services and customer experience

- Comply with relevant laws and regulations in Ghana

4. Protection of Personal Data

We implement security measures to protect personal data from:

- Unauthorized access

- Disclosure

- Destruction

These measures include:

- Firewalls

- Access controls

- Secure storage

5. Disclosure of Personal Data

We may disclose personal data to:

- Universities to verify student information

- Delivery services to deliver documents

- Regulatory bodies in Ghana, as required by law

We will not disclose personal data to third parties for marketing purposes without client consent.

6. Client Rights

Clients have the right to:

- Access their personal data

- Request deletion of their personal data

- Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Retention of Personal Data

We will retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with applicable laws in Ghana.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Clients will be notified of significant changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at UDP@gmail.com.
